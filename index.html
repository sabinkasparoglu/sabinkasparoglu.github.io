Sabin Kasparoglu, PhD

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at North Carolina State University, where I just had my Ph.D. degree on October 12, 2022, in Atmospheric Sciences. During my Ph.D., I was working on the physicochemical properties of secondary organic aerosols. My research interests are the measurement of viscosity and ice nucleation ability of aerosols and the characterization and design improvements of aerosol instrumentation. I received a B.S. degree in Chemical Engineering and an M.S. degree in Atmospheric Sciences from Istanbul Technical University in 2013 and 2017.
